## How to make japanese bacons crisp
1. Lay the bacons on the pan.
2. Pour water to cover the bacons and boil on high heat.
3. After the water boils, set the heat to medium, and if it evaporated completely, set the heat to low.
4. Heat for about 10 minutes while turning it over.
5. When the bacons becomes crisp, raise it to kitchen paper and remove the oil.
